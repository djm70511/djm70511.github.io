On their Fall Break many students from the University of Georgia traveled down Georgia Highway 15 all the way to St. Simons Island, GA to attend what is today known as "Frat Beach." This is a tradition unmatched by the Gators and a reason that the finest University in the Southeast will forever win the partying portion of the Georgia-Florida game. After countless "Callings of the Dawgs," and plenty of fun in St. Simon's the UGA students headed even further south to Jacksonville, FL. Although the game was lost, we once again showed our pride for the greatest university in all of the land, fighting it out through the last whistle.
